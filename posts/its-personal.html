Personal Finance.. It's Personal

Values

  • what beliefs motivate our actions?
  • before we can think about money and why we want to make a certain salary, we need to understand something much deeper
  • for example, I have always been a saver but it took me a long time before I could admit to myself that I was saving because I always wanted to have kids, and I knew it would be important to have money to raise them
  • before you can work on the money aspects of personal finance, you need to figure out and admit to yourself what's important to you
  • maybe you care a lot about travelling while you're young or retiring early
  • knowing what's important will help you with the next step of setting goals

Habits

  • before getting a credit card, for example, think about your habits and tendencies
  • maybe you have a habit of spending beyond what's in your account
  • or perhaps you don't have the habit of being organized, so you may forget to pay your bill some months
  • if you know yourself to be like this, maybe hold off on the credit card until you can improve your habits
  • be honest with yourself about your tendencies, so you can make better financial decisions

Lifestyle

  • what kind of lifestyle do you lead?
  • maybe you spend a lot of time socializing with friends over coffee or playing sports
  • is your spending in line with the lifestyle you imagine for yourself?
  • maybe your spending shows you go out to lots of clubs but in reality you don't enjoy doing that
  • take some time to think about what kind of lifestyle is important to you and look at your spending to see if it matches with what you think is important
